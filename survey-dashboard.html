Getting started

  1. Make your Google Sheet accessible: Share → Anyone with the link can view
  2. Copy the Sheet ID from the URL:
    docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/[SHEET-ID]/edit
  3. Paste the ID above and click Load from Google Sheets
  4. If your data is on a non-first tab, find the GID in the URL after #gid=

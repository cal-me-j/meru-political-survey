Meru County Political Survey Questionnaire

This survey seeks to gather the views and opinions of registered voters in Meru County. The primary goal is to assess the awareness and popularity of Key Actors to provide key insights for the party's strategic planning.
Your feedback is essential in ensuring that party decisions are guided by the actual needs and sentiments of the Meru electorate. All responses are strictly confidential.
